Exploratory Data Analysis

Data was taken from Kaggle, where roughly 6,000 tweets from each person was taken from most of 2016. Data was extensively cleaned up of retweets, and non-English tweets, punction marks, and blank lines.

A total of 3,090 and 2,556 Trump and Hillary tweets were left for the model to train and test

No, there are not the 2016 election results.
